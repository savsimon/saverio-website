Bio

Saverio Simonelli is Professor of Economics at the University of Naples Federico II and research fellow at CSEF. He holds a PhD in Economics from the University of Naples Federico II. His research focuses on fiscal policy, labor productivity, macroeconomic forecasting, and financial instability. He is a co-founder of Now-Casting.com.

  • 2018–current: Professor, Univ. of Naples Federico II
  • 2016–2017: Visiting Professor, Univ. of Mannheim
  • 2014–2018: Associate Professor, Univ. of Naples Federico II
  • 2005–2014: Assistant Professor, Univ. of Naples Federico II
  • 2003: PhD, Univ. of Naples Federico II
  • 2001: Visiting Scholar, Boston College
  • 2000: MSc, Universitat Pompeu Fabra
