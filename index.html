Bio

Saverio Simonelli is Professor of Economics at the University of Naples Federico II and research fellow at CSEF, and holds a PhD in Economics from the University of Naples Federico II. His current research focuses on fiscal policy, labor productivity, macroeconomic forecasting and instability of financial institutions. He is a co-founder of Now-Casting.com
